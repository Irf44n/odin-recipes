Brown Sugar Milk Tea

Description

Brown Sugar Milk Tea is a Taiwanese beverage that marries the indulgent sweetness of brown sugar with the creamy richness of milk tea. It's a delightful blend of black tea, warm caramelized brown sugar, and velvety milk, creating a harmonious symphony of flavors that's both comforting and utterly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Black tea leaves
  • Brown sugar
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tapioca pearls

Steps

  1. Brew black tea and let it cool.
  2. In a separate pot, melt brown sugar with water until it forms a syrup.
  3. Combine the brewed tea, brown sugar syrup, boiled tapioca pearls and milk.
  4. Stir well until everything is thoroughly mixed.
  5. Serve your Brown Sugar Milk Tea and enjoy!
