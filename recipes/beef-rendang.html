Beef Rendang

Description

Beef Rendang is an exquisite Indonesian culinary masterpiece that tantalizes the taste buds with its rich, aromatic, and irresistible flavors. Succulent chunks of tender beef are slow-cooked to perfection in a symphony of coconut milk, lemongrass, galangal, and a medley of exotic spices. The result is a mouthwatering fusion of creamy, spicy, and savory notes, harmoniously balanced to create a culinary journey. This delectable dish promises an unforgettable explosion of taste, making it an irresistible choice for those seeking a truly indulgent and savory experience.

Ingredients

  • Beef chunks
  • Coconut milk
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Cinnamon, cloves, star anise
  • Chillies
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Tamarind
  • Kaffir lime leaves

Steps

  1. Sear beef chunks until browned.
  2. Blend lemongrass, galangal, spices, chilies, shallots, and garlic.
  3. Cook the spice paste in a pan.
  4. Add beef, coconut milk, tamarind paste, and kaffir lime leaves.
  5. Simmer on low heat for hours until the beef is tender and the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve your delicious Beef Rendang!
